Technoblade Never Dies

Honoring a legend in Minecraft and in life

Technoblade's Journey

  • 2013: Started YouTube channel
  • 2015: Gained recognition for Hypixel PvP videos
  • 2019: Joined Minecraft Monday & SMP Earth
  • 2020: Invited to Famous Dream SMP & won the Potato War
  • 2021: Diagnosed with cancer, shared bravely with fans
  • 2022: Final video “so long nerds” released by family
  • 2022-Now: His friends and father (Technodad) continued to upload videos in his memory
